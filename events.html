Events

Our community events are the perfect opportunity to get involved, learn new skills, and celebrate the joys of gardening. From plant swaps and seasonal festivals to sustainability-focused discussions, our events connect gardeners, experts, and environmental enthusiasts to share ideas and foster a love for organic gardening.

Workshops

Our hands-on workshops provide in-depth training on a variety of gardening topics. Led by experienced gardeners and industry experts, these workshops cover everything from soil health and composting to growing herbs and vegetables in small spaces. Each session is designed to give you the practical skills and knowledge you need to make your garden thrive.

Private Events

Looking to host your own gardening event or private gathering? Private events at Sprout & Grow offer a unique, intimate experience for groups seeking personalized attention and expert guidance. Whether you're planning a garden party, team-building activity, or a special birthday celebration, we tailor our events to meet your needs, making sure every guest leaves inspired and equipped with new gardening knowledge.
